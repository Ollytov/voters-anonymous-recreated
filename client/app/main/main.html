Create a Poll!

How to Publish a Poll in 2 Steps

1. Create a Question

Type your question in the appropriate input area above. Fill out any possible options that you want to be included into the text fields under the question.

2. Click Create Poll

Click on the Create Poll button. This will save your poll and redirect you to the location of your newly created poll. where you can share it with your friends!
